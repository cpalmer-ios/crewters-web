## Register

<Callout>
  To test our Beta version of Crewters iOS, first sign up with your email and then download [Test Flight](https://play.tailwindcss.com/uj1vGACRJA?layout=preview) from the App Store. We will notify you by email once you have been registered with your download link. **We currently only support iOS.**
</Callout>

Once Crewters as been downloaded to your phone, open the app and you will see this screen: 

<div className="flex justify-center">
  <Image
    src="/images/screen7.png"
    width="200"
    height="200"
    alt="Sports App Crewters Login Screen"
  />
</div>

By default, Crewters Language is set to English (UK),select the drop down at the top to change the laguage to your choice. Then click **Register**.

For more information about our user authentication, [read the auth0 documentation](https://auth0.com/docs/authenticate).
